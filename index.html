Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our countertop services and process.

How long does installation take?

Typical installation takes 5-7 business days from the time of templating to final installation. We pride ourselves on our rapid turnaround times.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes! We provide completely free, no-obligation estimates. You can schedule a consultation online or give us a call to get started.

What is the difference between Quartz and Granite?

Granite is a natural stone with unique patterns and high heat resistance. Quartz is engineered, non-porous (doesn't need sealing), and offers more consistent patterns. Both are excellent durable choices.
