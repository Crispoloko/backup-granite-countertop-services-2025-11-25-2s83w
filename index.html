About Backup Granite Countertop Services

Founded in 2010, Backup Granite Countertop Services emerged from a simple vision: to provide homeowners and businesses with access to premium countertop materials at affordable prices without compromising quality. What started as a small family operation has grown into a trusted industry leader, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the Pacific Northwest. Our founders recognized a gap in the market where customers were forced to choose between luxury and affordability. Today, we've built our reputation on proving that you don't have to sacrifice one for the other. Our state-of-the-art fabrication facility and team of master craftspeople work tirelessly to deliver countertops that combine stunning aesthetics with exceptional durability and value.

At the heart of Backup's mission lies a commitment to excellence, integrity, and customer satisfaction. We believe that every home deserves beautiful, high-quality countertops that enhance both functionality and aesthetics. Our core values guide every decision we make: we prioritize transparency in pricing, maintain rigorous quality standards, invest in the latest technology and training for our team, and treat every customer relationship as a partnership. We're not just in the business of selling countertops; we're in the business of transforming spaces and exceeding expectations. By combining direct fabrication capabilities with expert design consultation and professional installation, we ensure that each project is completed to perfection. Our commitment to sustainability means we source materials responsibly and minimize waste, contributing to a better future for our communities and environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our countertop services, materials, and installation process.

Most of our projects are completed within 5-7 business days from the time of order. This includes fabrication and professional installation. The exact timeline depends on the complexity of your project and current order volume. We'll provide you with a specific timeline during your consultation. Rush options are available for an additional fee if you need your countertops sooner.

Maintenance varies by material. Quartz countertops require minimal maintenance simply wipe with warm soapy water and dry with a soft cloth. Granite countertops should be sealed annually and cleaned with pH-neutral cleaners. Marble requires more careful maintenance and should be sealed regularly. We provide detailed care instructions with every installation and our team is always available to answer maintenance questions. Most of our customers find that proper care keeps their countertops looking beautiful for decades.

Yes! We stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. All our countertops come with a standard 5-year warranty covering fabrication and installation defects. Our warranty covers any defects in workmanship or materials, ensuring your complete peace of mind with your investment.
